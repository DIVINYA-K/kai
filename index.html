What is Kairos?

Kairos is a organisation, providing free and healthy lunches for all kids!!

SUSHI BENTO

The Sushi Bento box contains 8 pieces of sushi of your choice and healthy snacks including popcorn and carrot sticks. It is a lovely and healthy meal for kids

WRAP BENTO

The Wrap Bento box contains 1 medium sized wrap with fillings of your choice and healthy snacks including nuts and yougurt. It is a wonderful healthy meal for kids

SALAD BENTO

The Salad Bento box contains a bowl of salad with grains, veggies and many good sources of protein. Snacks include pea crisps and healthy energy bars. This Bento is a great healthy meal choice.
